What exactly is UX?

UX stands for User Experience; so that means I specialize in professional empathy, I make design more human. My job goes beyond just making things look pretty, it's all about designing a wonderful and engaging experience for the consumer and tailoring web or mobile apps to their needs, but finding the sweet spot inbetween what the company is trying to say and what the consumer is supposed to understand.
